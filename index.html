Dhillon Business Students' Association (DBSA)

Recognizing the need for a centralized, engaging, and user-friendly platform for the DBSA (Designated Business Students’ Association), I took the initiative to design and develop a brand-new website from scratch. The new site functions as a landing page-style hub, providing students with seamless access to club information, events, and opportunities, while also catering to potential sponsors and external stakeholders. By integrating a modern UI, improved navigation, and a structured layout, the website enhances communication and fosters engagement within the student body. This project underscores my commitment to improving student experiences through innovative web solutions, ensuring that the DBSA remains accessible, professional, and connected to its members.
